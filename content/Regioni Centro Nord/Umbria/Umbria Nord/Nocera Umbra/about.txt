<p>Nocera Umbra, a hilltop town in Umbria’s Apennines, is known for its mineral springs and medieval heritage. Rebuilt after a 1997 earthquake, its historic center features the Romanesque Chiesa di San Francesco and the Torre Civica, offering valley views. A stop on the Via Francigena, Nocera’s thermal baths and hiking trails to Monte Pennino draw visitors. Its cuisine, including strangozzi pasta and truffles, reflects Umbrian bounty, making Nocera a serene destination for history and wellness seekers.</p>
