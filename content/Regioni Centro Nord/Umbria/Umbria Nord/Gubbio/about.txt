<p>Gubbio, Umbria’s medieval jewel, feels frozen in time with its Gothic palaces and Roman theater. The Piazza Grande, flanked by the Palazzo dei Consoli, offers sweeping views, while the annual Corsa dei Ceri, a thrilling candle race, pulses with tradition. Monte Ingino’s Basilica di Sant’Ubaldo, reached by cable car, houses the saint’s relics. Gubbio’s truffle-rich cuisine and ceramic craftsmanship, paired with its hauntingly beautiful streets, make it a must-visit for history buffs and romantics.</p>
