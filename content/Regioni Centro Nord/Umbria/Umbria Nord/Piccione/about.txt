<p>Piccione, a tiny hamlet near Perugia in Umbria, is a tranquil escape defined by its olive groves and pastoral landscapes. Its name, meaning “pigeon,” evokes its peaceful vibe, with scattered stone houses and small chapels like Santa Maria di Piccione. A hub for agriturismo, Piccione offers farm-to-table meals featuring Umbrian staples like wild boar and truffles. Its proximity to Perugia and Assisi makes it a perfect base for exploring Umbria’s cultural and culinary treasures in serene surroundings.</p>
