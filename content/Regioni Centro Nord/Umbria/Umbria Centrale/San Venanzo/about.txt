<p>San Venano, a hilltop village in Umbria’s Terni province, is a tranquil retreat known for its volcanic landscapes and medieval heritage. Its Castello di San Venanzo and Chiesa di San Venanzo Martire anchor the historic center, while the nearby Parco Vulcanico features geological trails. Local producers craft olive oil and Sagrantino wines, served with truffle dishes. San Venanzo’s quiet charm and proximity to Orvieto make it an ideal stop for those seeking Umbria’s lesser-known natural and cultural treasures.</p>
