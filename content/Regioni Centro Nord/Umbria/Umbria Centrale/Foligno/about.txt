<p>Foligno, Umbria’s commercial hub, pulses with Renaissance splendor and market-town energy. Its Piazza della Repubblica, flanked by the Romanesque Duomo and Palazzo Trinci’s frescoes, is a cultural heart, hosting the Giostra della Quintana, a baroque jousting event. The Calamita Cosmica, a quirky giant skeleton sculpture, adds modern intrigue. Foligno’s cuisine, from strangozzi al tartufo to local wines, shines, while its rail links to Assisi and Spoleto make it a convenient base for exploring Umbria’s treasures.</p>
