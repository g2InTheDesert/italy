<p>Capodacqua, a tiny hamlet in Umbria’s Foligno area, embodies rural serenity amid rolling hills and poppy fields. Its medieval church, Chiesa di Santa Maria, and scattered farmhouses reflect a timeless lifestyle. A stop for agriturismo, Capodacqua offers farm-fresh meals like lentil soups and roasted game, paired with local Grechetto. Its proximity to the Topino River and trails to Monte Subasio makes it a peaceful base for nature lovers exploring Umbria’s central countryside near Assisi.</p>
