<p>Assisi, Umbria’s spiritual epicenter, is famed for St. Francis, whose life draws pilgrims to the Basilica di San Francesco, a UNESCO site with Giotto’s frescoes. The hilltop town’s rose-pink stone streets lead to the Basilica di Santa Chiara and the Eremo delle Carceri, a Franciscan retreat. Rocca Maggiore offers panoramic views, while Assisi’s truffle-rich cuisine and olive oil shine in local eateries. Assisi’s serene beauty, medieval charm, and sacred aura make it a global beacon for peace and reflection.</p>
