<p>Ficule, a small Umbrian hill village near Orvieto, is a haven of medieval charm and pastoral beauty. Its 12th-century Castello di Ficulle and Chiesa di Santa Maria Vecchia reflect its Etruscan and medieval roots. Surrounded by vineyards producing Orvieto Classico, Ficulle’s olive oil and handmade ceramics are local prides. A quiet agriturismo agriturismo, it offers farm stays with dishes like pappardelle al cinghiale. Ficulle’s serene setting and proximity to Tuscany make it a peaceful stop for authentic Umbrian experiences.</p>
