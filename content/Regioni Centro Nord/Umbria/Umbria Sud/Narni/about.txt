<p>Narni, a medieval Umbrian hilltop town, inspired C.S. Lewis’s *Narnia* with its enchanting streets and mythic aura. Its Roman bridge, Ponte di Augusto, and the 12th-century Duomo di San Giovenale are highlights, while Narni Sotterranea reveals underground Roman and medieval secrets. The annual Corsa all’Anello revives medieval pageantry with jousting. Narni’s cuisine, featuring manfricoli pasta and wild boar, and its dramatic Tiber Valley views make it a captivating stop for history and fantasy lovers.</p>
