<p>Spoleto, a Umbrian hilltop city, is a cultural gem known for its Festival dei Due Mondi, a world-class arts event. Its Roman aqueduct, Ponte delle Torri, and the 12th-century Duomo, with Filippi’s frescoes, are stunning. The Rocca Albornoziana fortress offers panoramic views, while Roman ruins like the Teatro Romano add ancient depth. Spoleto’s cuisine, featuring black truffles and spelt risotto, shines. With its blend of history, art, and Umbrian charm, Spoleto is a vibrant destination for culture lovers.</p>
