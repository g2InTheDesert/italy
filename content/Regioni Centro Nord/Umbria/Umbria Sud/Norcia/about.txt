<p>Norcia, nestled in Umbria’s Sibillini Mountains, is a gastronomic and spiritual haven, birthplace of St. Benedict. Famed for its norcinerie (cured meats) and black truffles, its Piazza San Benedetto features a 13th-century basilica and Castellina fortress. Despite earthquake damage, Norcia’s resilience shines in its vibrant markets and festivals. Surrounded by hiking trails and wildflower meadows, its hearty cuisine and monastic heritage make Norcia a pilgrimage for foodies and nature enthusiasts seeking Umbria’s wild heart.</p>
