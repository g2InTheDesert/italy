<p>Orvieto, a dramatic Umbrian city atop a volcanic tuff, is famed for its Gothic Duomo, a 14th-century masterpiece with Luca Signorelli’s frescoes. The Pozzo di San Patrizio, a spiral well, and Orvieto Underground, a maze of Etruscan caves, fascinate visitors. Known for its crisp Orvieto Classico wine and wild boar dishes, the city hosts the Umbria Jazz Winter festival. Orvieto’s blend of art, history, and ethereal hilltop charm makes it a captivating stop in central Italy.</p>
