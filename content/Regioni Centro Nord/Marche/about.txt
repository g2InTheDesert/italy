<p>Arquata del Tronto, a resilient medieval village in the province of Ascoli Piceno, Marche, nestles in the scenic Tronto Valley, surrounded by the majestic Gran Sasso and Monti della Laga National Park. Renowned for its historic Rocca di Arquata and vibrant cultural traditions, this charming comune invites visitors to explore its rich heritage and natural splendor despite its ongoing recovery from the 2016 earthquake.</p>
