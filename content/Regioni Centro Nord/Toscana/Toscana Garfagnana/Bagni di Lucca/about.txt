<p>Bagni di Lucca, a historic spa town in Tuscany’s Garfagnana, is famed for its thermal springs, enjoyed since Roman times by nobility like Napoleon’s sister Elisa. Its elegant 19th-century architecture, including the Casino Reale and Villa Ada, reflects its glamorous past, while the Lima River offers kayaking. Surrounded by forests, it’s ideal for hiking to nearby villages like Montefegatesi. Bagni di Lucca’s blend of wellness, history, and outdoor adventure makes it a rejuvenating stop in northern Tuscany.</p>
