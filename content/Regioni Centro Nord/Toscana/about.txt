<p>Toscana (Tuscany) is Italy’s quintessential region, famed for its rolling hills, vineyards, and Renaissance cities. Florence, the birthplace of the Renaissance, houses masterpieces like Michelangelo’s David and Brunelleschi’s Duomo. Siena’s medieval charm shines during the Palio horse race, while Pisa’s Leaning Tower draws crowds. Tuscany’s countryside, dotted with cypress trees and hilltop towns like San Gimignano, produces world-class Chianti and Brunello wines. With its art, history, and farm-to-table cuisine, Toscana captivates visitors seeking beauty and culture.</p>
