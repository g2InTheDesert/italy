<p>Toscana Centrale, the heart of Tuscany, is a treasure trove of Renaissance art and medieval beauty. Florence, the cradle of the Renaissance, dazzles with its Duomo, Uffizi Gallery, and Michelangelo’s David. Siena’s Piazza del Campo hosts the thrilling Palio, while Pisa’s Leaning Tower iconicizes the skyline. San Gimignano’s medieval towers and Cortona’s hilltop charm add enchantment, alongside spiritual sites like La Verna. From Lucca’s walled city to Bibbiena’s Casentino Valley, this section offers a perfect blend of culture, history, and scenic splendor.</p>
