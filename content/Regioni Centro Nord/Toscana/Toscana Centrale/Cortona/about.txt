<p>Cortona, a hilltop Etruscan town in Tuscany’s Valdichiana, enchants with its steep medieval streets and panoramic views of Lake Trasimeno. Its Piazza della Repubblica, flanked by the Palazzo Comunale, pulses with life, while the MAEC museum showcases Etruscan treasures. The Diocesan Museum houses Fra Angelico’s masterpieces. Cortona’s fame from *Under the Tuscan Sun* draws romantics, and its olive oil, Chianina beef, and annual festivals like Giostra dell’Archidado add allure. Cortona’s timeless beauty and rustic cuisine make it a Tuscan classic.</p>
