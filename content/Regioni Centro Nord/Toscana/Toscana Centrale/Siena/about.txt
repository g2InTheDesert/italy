<p>Siena, Tuscany’s Gothic masterpiece, captivates with its medieval streets and the iconic Piazza del Campo, where the Palio horse race electrifies crowds twice yearly. The Duomo, a black-and-white marble marvel, houses works by Bernini and Donatello, while the Torre del Mangia offers sweeping views. Siena’s contrade (neighborhoods) foster fierce local pride, evident in its festivals and cuisine, like panforte and pici. A UNESCO site, Siena’s timeless beauty and vibrant traditions make it a cultural jewel of central Italy.</p>
