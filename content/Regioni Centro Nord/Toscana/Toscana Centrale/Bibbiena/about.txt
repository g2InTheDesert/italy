<p>Bibbiena, a historic town in Tuscany’s Casentino Valley, blends medieval charm with lush forested landscapes. Its Palazzo Dovizi and the Sanctuary of Santa Maria del Sasso reflect its Renaissance and spiritual heritage, while the nearby Casentino Forests National Park offers hiking amid ancient woodlands. Bibbiena’s annual Rievocazione della Mea revives medieval traditions, and its cuisine, featuring tortelli di patate, celebrates local ingredients. A gateway to Tuscany’s lesser-known east, Bibbiena is perfect for those seeking history and nature off the tourist trail.</p>
