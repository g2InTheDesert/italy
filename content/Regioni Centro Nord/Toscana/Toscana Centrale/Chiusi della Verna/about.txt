<p>Chiusi della Verna, a small village near La Verna in Tuscany’s Casentino Valley, is steeped in Franciscan history and natural splendor. Its stone houses and forested hills surround the sacred La Verna sanctuary, creating a peaceful backdrop. The village’s Chiesa di San Michele and local festivals honor its medieval roots, while trails lead to panoramic viewpoints. Known for chestnut dishes and wild mushrooms, Chiusi della Verna offers rustic hospitality, making it a quiet retreat for pilgrims and nature lovers exploring Tuscany’s spiritual heart.</p>
