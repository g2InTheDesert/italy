<p>Pisa, Tuscany’s coastal gem, is synonymous with the Leaning Tower, a quirky marvel in the Campo dei Miracoli, alongside the Romanesque Duomo and Baptistery. Beyond its UNESCO-listed piazza, Pisa’s Arno River promenade and lively student vibe, fueled by its ancient university, create a vibrant atmosphere. The Knights’ Square and Santa Maria della Spina’s Spina’s Gothic church add historic depth, while local seafood and cecina (chickpea flatbread) delight. Pisa’s blend of iconic sights, youthful energy, and riverside charm makes it a captivating stop.</p>
