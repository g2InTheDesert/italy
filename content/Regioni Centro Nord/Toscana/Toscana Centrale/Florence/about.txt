<p>Florence, Tuscany’s Renaissance cradle, is a global art capital, home to Michelangelo’s David, Botticelli’s *Birth of Venus*, and Brunelleschi’s iconic Duomo dome. The Uffizi Gallery and Palazzo Vecchio showcase unparalleled masterpieces, while the Ponte Vecchio spans the Arno River with golden charm. Florence’s leather markets, vibrant piazzas, and gelaterias buzz with life, and its cuisine, from bistecca alla fiorentina to ribollita, is hearty. A city where every corner holds history, Florence is an essential destination for art, culture, and romance.</p>
