<p>Montepulciano, a Renaissance hilltop town in southern Tuscany, is famed for its Vino Nobile wine and elegant architecture. Piazza Grande, flanked by the Palazzo Comunale and Duomo, is its heart, offering panoramic views of Val d’Orcia. Historic cantinas, carved into the town’s foundations, invite wine tastings, while the San Biagio church, a Renaissance masterpiece, lies below. Montepulciano’s blend of noble heritage, cinematic charm (featured in *Twilight*), and rich cuisine makes it a must-visit for Tuscan enthusiasts.</p>
