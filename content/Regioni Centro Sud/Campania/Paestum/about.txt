<p>Paestum, an archaeological marvel in Campania, boasts some of the world’s best-preserved Greek temples, dating to the 6th century BC. The Doric temples of Hera, Neptune, and Athena stand majestically amid lush plains, offering a glimpse into Magna Graecia’s grandeur. The adjacent museum houses treasures like the Tomb of the Diver fresco. Surrounded by fields producing famed mozzarella di bufala, Paestum combines ancient history with culinary delights, making it a must-visit for history buffs and food lovers exploring southern Italy.</p>
