<p>Ravello, perched high above the Amalfi Coast, is a refined retreat known for its breathtaking views and cultural legacy. Its lush gardens, like those of Villa Rufolo and Villa Cimbrone, inspired artists like Wagner and offer panoramic vistas of the sea. The town’s Duomo di San Pantaleone, with its bronze doors, anchors the elegant piazza, while the Ravello Festival celebrates music amid historic settings. Ravello’s tranquil sophistication, paired with its romantic landscapes, makes it a haven for those seeking beauty and inspiration.</p>
