<p>Castellabate, a charming hilltop village in Campania’s Cilento region, overlooks the Tyrrhenian Sea with timeless serenity. Its UNESCO-listed medieval center, with cobblestone streets and stone houses, exudes authenticity, while the nearby Santa Maria di Castellabate offers pristine beaches like Punta Licosa. Popularized by the film *Benvenuti al Sud*, the village retains its quiet charm, with local trattorias serving rustic dishes like fusilli al ragù. Castellabate’s blend of coastal beauty, historic ambiance, and unspoiled nature makes it a hidden gem for slow travel.</p>
