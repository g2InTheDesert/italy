<p>Napoli, Campania’s pulsating capital, is a chaotic yet captivating city where history, art, and grit collide. The birthplace of pizza, its bustling Spaccanapoli street brims with baroque churches, street food, and vibrant markets. Highlights include the Napoli Sotterranea, an underground Roman network, and the Museo Archeologico Nazionale, housing Pompeii’s treasures. The Royal Palace and Castel Nuovo reflect its regal past, while Vesuvius looms nearby. Napoli’s raw energy, soulful cuisine, and cultural depth make it an unmissable, if intense, Italian experience.</p>
