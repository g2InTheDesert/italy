<p>Campania, a vibrant southern region, dazzles with its Amalfi Coast, where towns like Positano, Amalfi, and Ravello cascade down cliffs to turquoise seas. Naples, the birthplace of pizza, pulses with baroque art and chaotic charm. Ancient Greek temples at Paestum rival Pompeii’s ruins, while Sorrento’s lemon groves and Salerno’s historic center add coastal allure. Campania’s cuisine, from mozzarella di bufala to sfogliatella, is legendary, making it a magnet for romantics, historians, and food lovers.</p>
