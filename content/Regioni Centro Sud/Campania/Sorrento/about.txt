<p>Sorrento, a cliffside town overlooking the Bay of Naples, is a gateway to the Amalfi Coast, famed for its lemon groves and vibrant charm. Its historic center, with narrow lanes lined by artisan shops selling limoncello and inlaid woodwork, buzzes with life. Piazza Tasso, the heart of town, is perfect for people-watching, while Marina Grande’s colorful fishing boats add seaside allure. Sorrento’s proximity to Capri and Pompeii, paired with its lively yet relaxed vibe, makes it a beloved base for exploring Campania’s treasures.</p>
