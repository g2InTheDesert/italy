<p>Amalfi, a jewel of the Amalfi Coast, is a historic maritime town nestled between towering cliffs and the turquoise Tyrrhenian Sea. Once a powerful maritime republic in the Middle Ages, its iconic Duomo di Sant’Andrea, with its striped Byzantine facade and soaring bell tower, dominates the central piazza. Narrow, winding streets brim with artisan shops selling ceramics and limoncello, while the town’s beaches offer Mediterranean bliss. Amalfi’s blend of history, vibrant local culture, and breathtaking coastal scenery makes it a romantic haven for travelers seeking la dolce vita.</p>
