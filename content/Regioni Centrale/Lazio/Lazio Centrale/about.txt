<p>Lazio Centrale, the pulsating core of Italy, is dominated by Rome, the Eternal City, where ancient landmarks like the Colosseum, Pantheon, and Roman Forum blend with vibrant modern life. Vatican City, an independent enclave within Rome, draws millions to St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel. Beyond the capital, Tivoli enchants with its historic Villa d’Este, famed for its Renaissance gardens and fountains, offering a serene escape. This section combines unparalleled historical depth, spiritual significance, and cultural richness, making it a global beacon for travelers.</p>
