<p>Tivoli, a historic hilltop town east of Rome, is famed for its UNESCO-listed treasures: Villa d’Este, with its dazzling Renaissance gardens and fountains, and Hadrian’s Villa, a sprawling 2nd-century imperial retreat. The town’s medieval center, with the Rocca Pia fortress, overlooks the Aniene River’s waterfalls. Tivoli’s travertine quarries and cuisine, like pizzutello grapes and gnocchi, add local flavor. A short trip from Rome, Tivoli’s blend of ancient ruins and Renaissance splendor </p>captivates history and art lovers.
