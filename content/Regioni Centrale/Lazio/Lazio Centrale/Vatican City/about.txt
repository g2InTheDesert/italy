<p>Vatican City, the world’s smallest state, is the spiritual heart of Catholicism, centered on St. Peter’s Basilica, with its awe-inspiring dome and Michelangelo’s Pietà. The Vatican Museums house treasures like the Sistine Chapel’s frescoes by Michelangelo and Raphael’s Stanze. The Swiss Guard and papal ceremonies add pageantry, while St. Peter’s Square buzzes with pilgrims. A sovereign enclave in Rome, Vatican City’s unparalleled art, history, and sacred aura make it a global pilgrimage site.</p>
