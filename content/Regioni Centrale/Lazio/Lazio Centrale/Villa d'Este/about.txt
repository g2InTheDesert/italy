<p>Villa d’Este, a Renaissance masterpiece in Tivoli, Lazio, is renowned for its spectacular terraced gardens and over 500 fountains, including the Fontana dell’Ovato and the Hundred Fountains. Built for Cardinal Ippolito d’Este in the 16th century, its frescoed palace and hydraulic engineering marvels reflect opulence. A UNESCO site, the villa’s romantic ambiance, cascading waters, and panoramic views make it a must-visit for those exploring Lazio’s artistic and horticultural heritage near Rome.</p>
