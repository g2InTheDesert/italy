<p>Montefiascone, a hilltop town above Lake Bolsena in Lazio, is famed for its Est! Est!! Est!!! wine, born from a medieval legend. Its Romanesque Duomo di Santa Margherita, with one of Italy’s largest domes, and the Rocca dei Papi offer panoramic lake views. The town’s medieval streets host wine festivals, paired with dishes like coregone fish. Montefiascone’s blend of viticulture, history, and lakeside proximity makes it a delightful stop for wine lovers exploring northern Lazio.</p>
