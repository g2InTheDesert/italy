<p>Orte, a strategic hilltop town in Lazio’s Tiber Valley, boasts Etruscan roots and a medieval underground network, Orte Sotterranea, with ancient cisterns and tunnels. Its Romanesque Duomo and Palazzo Alberti reflect its layered past, while the annual Ottavario di Sant’Egidio festival lights up the streets. Surrounded by olive groves, Orte’s cuisine features fettuccine al ragù and local wines. A quiet gem near Viterbo, Orte offers history and authenticity for travelers seeking Lazio’s lesser-known treasures.</p>
