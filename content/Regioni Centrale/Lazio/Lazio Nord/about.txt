<p>Lazio Nord, a region of medieval charm and natural beauty, boasts picturesque towns like Viterbo, known as the “City of Popes” for its papal palace, and Bolsena, set on the tranquil Lake Bolsena. Bagnoregio, the “Dying City,” captivates with its near-abandoned medieval core, while Montefiascone offers panoramic views and fine wines. Other gems like Nepi and Orte showcase Etruscan and Roman heritage, and Castel Sant’Angelo’s lakeside fortress adds historic allure. Lazio Nord is a haven for those seeking history, quaint villages, and serene landscapes.</p>
