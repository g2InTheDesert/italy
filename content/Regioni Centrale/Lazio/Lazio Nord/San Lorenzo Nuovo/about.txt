<p>San Lorenzo Nuovo, a planned 18th-century town near Lake Bolsena in Lazio, is known for its orderly grid and neoclassical elegance. Its Piazza Europa, with the Chiesa di San Lorenzo, overlooks the lake, offering serene views. A hub for lentil and potato festivals, the town’s cuisine includes lake fish and olive oil. With beaches and hiking trails nearby, San Lorenzo Nuovo’s unique urban design and tranquil lakeside setting make it a charming stop in northern Lazio.</p>
