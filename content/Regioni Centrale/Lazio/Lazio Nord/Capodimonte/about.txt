<p>Capodimonte, a picturesque village on Lake Bolsena’s peninsula in northern Lazio, is a serene lakeside retreat. Its 15th-century Rocca Farnese dominates the skyline, while the medieval fishing harbor buzzes with summer activity. Known for its white wines and lake fish, Capodimonte’s trattorias serve dishes like sbroccata, a fish soup. With beaches, water sports, and proximity to Viterbo, Capodimonte offers a blend of history, nature, and relaxation for families and lake lovers.</p>
