<p>Lazio, the cradle of Roman civilization, is synonymous with Rome, Italy’s capital, where ancient wonders like the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Pantheon coexist with vibrant modern life. Beyond Rome, Lazio offers diverse attractions, from the serene Castelli Romani hill towns to the Etruscan necropolises of Tarquinia. The region’s coastline, including beaches at Ostia, and natural sites like Lake Bracciano, add to its appeal. Lazio’s culinary scene shines with classics like carbonara and cacio e pepe, making it a cultural and gastronomic hub that draws millions annually.</p>
