<p>Martinsicuro, a coastal town in Abruzzo’s Teramo province, is a relaxed Adriatic seaside resort known for its sandy beaches and family-friendly vibe. Its medieval Torre di Carlo V, a 16th-century watchtower, and the nearby Villa Rosa add historic charm. The Lungomare promenade buzzes with seafood restaurants serving brodetto and arrosticini. A hub for cycling along the Corridoio Verde Adriatico, Martinsicuro’s warm waters and proximity to the Gran Sasso make it a versatile summer destination.</p>
